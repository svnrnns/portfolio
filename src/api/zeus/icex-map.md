## Overview

ICEX, the Spanish Institute for Foreign Trade, is the principal Spanish government body dedicated to facilitating the internationalization of Spanish enterprises. It offers comprehensive programs including information dissemination, training, promotion, and specialized support under the Ministry of Economy, Trade, and Enterprise of Spain.

In response to ICEX's need for an integrated solution to streamline the management of national and international agreements, Zeus developed a tailored application. This project was completed efficiently within a remarkably short timeframe of just 14 days.

## Project Achievement

In this project, my task was to develop the frontend of the application to meet ICEX's requirements within a two-week period. The result was an application of exceptional quality, exceeding my previous work in both functionality and design.
