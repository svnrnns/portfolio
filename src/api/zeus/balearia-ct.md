# Balearia Control Tower

**Disclaimer**: This article does not contain any images in accordance with Zeus by LLYC privacy policies.

## Overview

Balearia is one of the largest companies in Spain, dominating the Mediterranean Sea with its control over travel mainly between the mainland and the Balearic Islands. In partnership with Zeus, Balearia has developed a sophisticated control tower—a data management system designed to monitor its fleet in real time.

## Features

The new version of the application provides:

- **Real-Time Ship Monitoring:** Visual tracking of ships on a world map.
- **Trip Schedules:** Access to detailed schedules for planning and management.
- **Ship Consumption Monitoring:** Tools to track and optimize fuel and resource usage.
- **Business Tracking:** Insights and analytics to support strategic decision-making.
