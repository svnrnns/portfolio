After finishing my studies, I had the unique opportunity to bring data to life for some of Spain's largest brands by working with Zeus, LLYC's business intelligence and data visualization platform. Zeus specializes in cleaning, capturing, and organizing data to provide companies a centralized, real-time view of their performance, often through visually appealing dashboards.

Each project with Zeus was a journey of creating clarity and connection through data. For instance, we helped Balearia monitor their fleet in real time, optimize fuel consumption, and provide insights and analytics to support strategic decision-making, among other features. I created unique interfaces for many similar projects, including those for Sesame HR, Telefónica, and Astara, all of which met the highest quality standards within record timelines, boosting project profitability by over 50%.

I also worked on non-data-monitoring related projects at Zeus, such as creating the entire university portal for the Gaias initiative by USFQ. We developed a in-depth process for students to generate, manage, and pay for requests to the various programs offered by Gaias. The application also included a highly flexible backoffice, giving Gaias staff complete control over all the programs and student data.

In my role as project lead, I managed both Backend and Frontend initiatives, optimizing team workflows with effective resource and time management. These efforts paid off, increasing team productivity by 20-30% and making teamwork as smooth as the products we built.

Over time, I also developed a suite of internal tools that simplified the complexities of development, making projects faster to build and higher in quality. These tools became essential, transforming how our teams approached their work and setting a new standard for what we could achieve together.
