# Ucersa Dashboard

**Disclaimer**: This article does not contain any images in accordance with Zeus by LLYC privacy policies.

## Overview

Ucersa, a Spanish company specializing in the manufacturing and distribution of electronic components, focuses on developing high-tech products for various industrial sectors. The company is known for its innovative and high-quality solutions.

The introduction of Zeus has marked a significant transformation in how Ucersa manages its internal operations. The new application provides employees with real-time monitoring capabilities, offering a detailed view of ongoing activities and processes throughout the organization.

## Project Achievements

This is one of the oldest ongoing projects at Zeus, and it continues to evolve to this day. My contributions to the project include the development of three dashboards within Ucersa's application, including a tool remarkably similar to modern Google Calendar functionalities.

This project presented an exciting challenge due to its technical requirements and the need to align with Zeus' vision for Ucersa's monitoring tool. The final result is a user interface that combines aesthetic appeal with effectiveness, enhancing business decision-making by visualizing and presenting critical data.
